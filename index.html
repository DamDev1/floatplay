About FloatPlay

FloatPlay is a Chrome extension designed to allow users to watch YouTube videos in Picture-in-Picture mode. This feature enables you to enjoy your favorite videos while multitasking or browsing other tabs.

With FloatPlay, you can easily toggle the PiP mode with a single click, providing a seamless viewing experience without interruptions.
