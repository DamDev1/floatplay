Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy page for FloatPlay. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information we do not collect and how we safeguard user privacy when using our extension.

Information We Do Not Collect

FloatPlay does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information such as name, email, or phone number is collected.
  • No browsing history or video viewing behavior is tracked.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used in the extension.
  • No user data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions Used

FloatPlay only requests the permissions necessary to enable Picture-in-Picture (PiP) for YouTube videos. Specifically:

  • activeTab Permission: This is required to enable PiP mode on the active YouTube tab when the user interacts with the extension.
  • YouTube Host Permission: This limits the extension's access to YouTube content only, ensuring it does not interact with other websites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be modified accordingly.
